Hi, there!


I’m Faridat, a UX Designer from Massachusetts!


In the past, I’ve worked as a commissioner for an international Presidential campaign, as a healthcare worker during Covid-19 and as a celebrity fashion stylist.


I’ve spent my days listening to people’s stories and emphasizing with them to offer creative solutions for their needs.


Ultimately, my goal is to merge my background in African studies and public health and use human centered design to drive creative, sustainable, high impact solutions for developmental challenges in the U.S. and abroad.
